# SuperStore-Dashboard
Interactive Tableau Dashboard with Sales Analysis
# Business Dashboard Task 3

## Objective
Design an interactive Tableau dashboard for business stakeholders to analyze sales, profit, and growth metrics.

## Dataset
Superstore Sales Data (Kaggle).

## Steps Taken
1. **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing**
   - Checked for missing values and duplicates
   - Converted date columns to Date format
   - Verified numeric columns like Sales, Profit, Quantity

2. **Dashboard Design & Visualizations**
   - **KPIs / Cards:** Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Quantity, Profit Margin
   - **Column Chart / Bar Chart / Pie Chart / Treemap**: Sales and Profit by Category, Region, Product
   - **Scatter Plot / Bubble Chart / Lollipop Chart**: Relationship between Profit vs Sales, Top Customers/Products
   - **Motion Chart / Forecast**: Sales trends over time
   - **Funnel / Waterfall Charts**: Order process insights, cumulative profit analysis
   - **Interactive Dashboard**: Combined multiple visualizations with filters and slicers for Region, Category, Year
   - Applied consistent **color theme** and formatting for clarity

3. **Insights / Key Takeaways**
   - Highest sales in [Region/Category]
   - Profit margins vary across categories
   - Top products driving revenue: [Product Names]
   - Seasonal trends observed in sales over the years
   - Forecast shows potential growth trends for next periods
